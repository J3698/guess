Welcome to Avatar Guess Who. The goal of the game is to guess the opponent's character before they guess yours.

Ask a yes or no question by typing it into the text input and clicking "ask". The answer/question will appear after your opponent answers the question. Be careful, the question and answer will not re-appear.

To toggle a character as eliminated, click his or her icon. In the smaller grid, you can see what characters your opponent has currently toggled.

When you're ready to guess your opponent's character, click the "guess" button. You can then click a character to guess, or click the guess button again to cancel. Be careful, if you guess wrong, you lose!
